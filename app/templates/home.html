How I Thought while designing Trading Systems at QuantradeAI

  • Design for failure before scale
  • Prefer explicit state transitions over implicit logic
  • Keep order execution deterministic and auditable
  • Use asynchronous processing to isolate external dependencies
  • Treat databases and queues as first-class system components
  • Avoid tight coupling between signal, execution, and notification layers
